Frequently asked

Is this a real AI or a recording?

It’s a live AI voice agent powered by our Geoteknik knowledge base — every call is unique.

Will it know my license / project details?

This public demo doesn’t look up your account. For account-specific help, the agent will hand off to a human.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. Use a recent Chrome, Safari, or Edge browser and grant microphone permission.

What if it can’t solve my problem?

It will capture your issue and route it to our engineering support team automatically.
